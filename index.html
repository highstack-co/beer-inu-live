Beeronomics

The $BEER smart contract features the following:

  • 12-22% redistribution in (pegged)Ethereum
  • Redistribution is automatically redistributed. No need for claming!t

‍

Every transaction, 12% goes to ETH redistribution, 2% goes to the beer wallet and 1% goes to the dev wallet. You will need at least 15% slippage to buy.

‍

Please note, as there are no bots you will not be frontrun and you can use high slippage without any risk.

‍

The token supply is 1,000,000,000,000. No extra tokens will ever be minted.

All the existing holders of ERC-20 $BEER could participate in the tokenswap. BSC $BEER has been distributed to the eligble participants.

The token supply will be divided as follows:

  • 4% community lottery wallet
  • 3% community action wallet
  • 1% charity partnership wallet
  • 92% to liquidity

The lottery and action wallet will reward a community buyer and a community activist in pegged ETH on numerous occasions

‍

Hangover Tax – Hodl your beer!

‍

$BEER aims to build a longterm community and a place for people to chill and relax. That is why $BEER will have an extra hangover tax: 20% for selling within the week, 10% for selling within the month. These extra taxes will be redistributed automatically between the holders in (pegged) ETH!

‍

Why the hangover tax? At $BEER we know we can only win big when we hit a high market cap. We will provide maximum incentive to hold. Enjoy the ETH redistribution on the way to the moon!


Please note that to sell BEER within the stage 1 hangover tax you will need to use Beerswap! We are currently working on migrating the BEERSWAP to handle the BSC-network
